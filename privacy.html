Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you register on Exalyte, we collect:

  • Your full name and email address
  • Encrypted password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Exam attempt data including scores and answers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for:

  • Providing examination services and score tracking
  • Administering your account and premium access
  • Improving platform performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely on Cloudflare D1 with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 before storage. We never sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies

Exalyte uses localStorage to maintain your authentication session. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@exalyte.com.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, email privacy@exalyte.com.
